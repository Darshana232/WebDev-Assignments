Delicious Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté garlic.
  3. Add cherry tomatoes and cook until softened.
  4. Mix in cooked pasta and season with salt & pepper.
  5. Top with grated cheese and serve warm.

Enjoy your meal! ❤️
